Death Cast was founded in 2011 as a way to give people the chance to say goodbye before they die.
Ever since then, a feeling of gratefulness washes over citizens when they pick up the phone;
and hear those beautiful words:

I regret to inform you that sometime in the next twenty-four hours you'll be meeting an untimely death.

Decker
A person who has recieved a phone call from Death-Cast, stating they will be dying in 24hours
Count Downers
A website, similar to Instagram and YouTube
Users are able to watch videos, view photos, and read blogposts, published by deckers during their final hours
Make-A-Moment
A virtual reality (VR) experience, allowing deckers to participate in extreme activities without any of the danger
Last Friend
A website that pairs up deckers with another user to spend their last day with
The other user can be a decker as well, or a non-decker
This is orignally a mobile app similar to bumble, but you can use the desktop application as well
Necro
A mobile app similar to Last Friend
Instead of deckers being paired with someone to spend their final days with, deckers are paired with a No Strings Attached hook-up partner
