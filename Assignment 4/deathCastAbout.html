Death Cast was founded in 2011 as a way to give people the chance to say goodbye before they die.

Ever since then, a feeling of gratefulness washes over citizens when they pick up the phone

and hear those beautiful words:

I regret to inform you that sometime in the next twenty-four hours you'll be meeting an untimely death.
